In this lab, I attempted to enhance machine learning model performance through optimal hyperparameter identification while evaluating manual tuning approaches against AutoML automation. I conducted my research by 
implementing Grid Search and Random Search methods to explore various hyperparameter combinations and assess their impact on model performance. I utilized AutoML which automated the process of training multiple 
algorithms and testing their performance through capacity optimization. The results demonstrated that the tuned models outperformed default models whereas the AutoML system achieved its best results through its 
ability to test multiple combinations and create ensemble models automatically. The study showed that hyperparameter optimization improves predictive accuracy while AutoML enables users to develop competitive models 
in less time.
